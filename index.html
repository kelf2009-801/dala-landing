Manifesto

Knowledge isn't stored.
It's distributed.

The answer you need is rarely in one place. It lives across Slack threads, meeting notes, Confluence pages, and the senior engineer who's seen this outage before. Dala doesn't move that knowledge into a new system — it reaches across every place it already lives and brings back the answer with the sources attached.

How it works

Ask once.
Reach everyone who knew.

Type a question the way you'd ask a teammate. Dala pulls from the living context of your organization — who worked on what, which doc was cited in the last incident, which decision got made and why. You get an answer and a trail: here's what I used, here's who I'd ask next, here's the edge I'm not certain about.
